FORT SANTIAGO, INTRAMUROS
  • Luzon
  • Found in Manila, Philippined
  • Best known for its rich history with the Spanish colonizers
  • Long ago, this was once the biggest pueblo (a place where the Spaniards relocated the Filipinos) in the Philippines. Many were killed, and many were tortured.
  • Until today, it is known for its bloodtstained history and being one of the most haunted places in the Philippines

  • Mindanao
  • Found in Iligan City, Lanao del Norte, Philippines
  • Known as the tallest waterfall in the Philippines
  • Could be traveled by private or public vehicles
  • There is a myth that the contact of the waterfall to the lake below it is so strong that it conducts electricity
